Information

Common Inquiries

Everything you need to know about our bespoke furniture process.

design_services How do I start a custom sofa order?

The process begins with a consultation where we discuss your space, style preferences, and comfort requirements. You can visit our showroom or contact us via call/Instagram to share your initial ideas.

history What is the typical lead time for a custom piece?

Generally, bespoke sofas take between 2 to 4 weeks to manufacture depending on the complexity of the design and availability of the selected fabric. We always prioritize quality over speed to ensure a perfect finish.

texture Can I choose my own fabric and cushions?

Absolutely. We offer a vast library of premium fabrics in various textures and colors. You can also customize the firmness of your cushions—from soft cloud-like comfort to firm orthopedic support.

description What is the 'Order Base' process?

'Order Base' means we don't just sell pre-made stock. Every item is built from the ground up based on a specific customer's order. This ensures your furniture is unique and perfectly suited to your specific interior dimensions.
